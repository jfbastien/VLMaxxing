\section{Automated Assets and Figure Sources}

The manuscript tooling automates paper elements with checked-in inputs: the routing holdout figure and table, headline
anti-recomputation assets, measured sparse-vision tables, C-PERSIST repair and
sampler/many-turn/setup-economics tables, the random-keep sanity table, the
scale-out bundle status table, memory
characterization, paired-drift summaries, and repo provenance generated
from the current checkout.

The sync step reads the checked artifact summaries used by this repo. Candidate
C-STREAM rows are generated from the checked scale-out bundle and remain
visibly bounded when the bundle is mixed.

Future novelty-ranked dense and native-streaming quality tables should follow
the same rule: generate from checked rows, not from free-form markdown or
screenshots.
