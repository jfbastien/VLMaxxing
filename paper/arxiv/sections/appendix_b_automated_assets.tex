\section{Automated Assets and Figure Sources}

The manuscript tooling automates only the paper elements that have stable,
checked-in inputs: the Lane~A holdout figure and table, headline
anti-recomputation assets, measured sparse-vision tables, C-PERSIST repair and
sampler tables, memory characterization, paired-drift summaries, and draft repo
provenance generated from the current checkout.

The sync step deliberately does not read sibling checkouts or free-form
deployment markdown. Candidate C-STREAM and scale-out rows remain manual and
visibly pending until a validated local artifact bundle lands. That keeps the
draft useful for incoming experiments without letting stale screenshots or
partner prose become release evidence.
