\section{Manuscript Completion Checklist}

This checklist is a release-freeze guard for the draft artifact, not evidence
for an additional claim. The manuscript can keep moving while new experiments
land, but a public source bundle should satisfy each item before the generated
provenance is treated as final.
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
\item Freeze the target venue template and stop building on the temporary draft style.
\item Replace any visible draft-note boxes with final prose, or move the note to
  the internal paper checklist if the underlying science is still open.
\item Rerun, demote, or drop supplementary rows that do not meet the
  paper-grade provenance bar.
\item Regenerate tables and figures from canonical artifacts wherever stable
  artifact paths exist.
\item Keep clean, advisory, artifact-pending, and dev-only evidence
  visibly distinct in the final manuscript and source traceability appendix.
\item Finalize the public code, artifact, and data-release wording.
\item Build the final arXiv source bundle from a clean tree and verify that the
  generated provenance reports the release commit without a dirty suffix.
\end{itemize}
